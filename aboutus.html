About Us

The Dandy Brewing Company is Calgary's original small brewery. Although it has grown a little, it's still small at heart. We produce inspired ales, with lots of care, in small batches.

Our unique approach to brewing and keen eye to every detail fosters creativity in both our recipes and beer styles.

On site, we are pleased to offer a unique brewing experience, featuring a full service tasting room with an exquisite menu of fine dining drinking food, a curated selection of cocktails, glass pours of selected wines, and 20 taps of Dandy beers.

We share a passion for not only brewing, but the people, experiences, and personal touches that make the real ale experience so special.
